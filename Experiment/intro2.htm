Slot Selection

To select the slot of your choice, use your <%expressions.buttoninstruct1%> to select one of the four squares.

Keep in mind: You only have <%parameters.selectionTimeout/1000%>s to make your choice. If you are too slow, a red X is flashed onto the screen to let you know that you missed your chance to get more points during this trial.


Press START to play a quick demo game so that you get familiar with the game set-up.
